Coconut & Turmeric Baked Chicken Thighs

Oven baked chicken and rice to try tonight

  • 6-8 Chicken Thighs
  • Salt
  • Turmeric
  • Cumin
  • Ginger
  • Ginger
  • Ginger
  • Coconut Milk
  • Spring Greens
  1. Grab a bowl and put in salt, turmeric, cumin, garlic, ginger and 3 tablespoons of coconut millk. Place chicken thighs in, rub and marinate for 15 minutes minumum
  2. Heat oven to 160C. Scatter onion and spring green to a baking tray plus rest of the coconut milk and maple syrup. Cook in oven for 10 minutes. Stir occasionaly. Place marinated chicken thighs on top and oven bake for 40 minutes, stirring occasionally
  3. Cook rice to serve with
  4. Grill for additional 8-10 minutes to crisp skin
